Carbon Emission Standards per Kilometer：

  • Low (< 100g/km): For the vast majority of pure electric vehicles (EVs)—even in regions where coal-fired power dominates—the average figure typically remains below 100g.
  • Med (100g-200g/km): Hybrid vehicles (Mix) or highly fuel-efficient, small-displacement gasoline vehicles.
  • High (> 200g/km): Large gasoline vehicles (Gasoline)，such as the Porsche 911 or large SUVs.
